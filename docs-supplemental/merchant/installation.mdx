## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase Theme">
    Purchase Khors Theme from Shopify Theme Store or our website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Store">
    In Shopify Admin, go to **Online Store > Themes > Add theme**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Theme">
    Click **Publish** to make Khors your active theme.
  </Step>
</Steps>
