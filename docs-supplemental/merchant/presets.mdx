## Available Presets

### Aura (Fashion / Clothing)

**Industry:** Clothing, Apparel, Fashion

The **Aura** preset is designed for clothing and fashion stores, offering an elegant, minimalist design that lets products shine.

**Key features:**
- Neutral color palette with bold accents
- Elegant serif or sophisticated sans-serif typography
- Large, high-quality product images
- Generous whitespace for premium feel
- Subtle animations and transitions

**Ideal for:**
- Clothing stores
- Fashion boutiques
- Streetwear brands
- Fashion accessories
- Sustainable fashion

**Shopify Industry Tag:** `Clothing`

---

### Apex (Beauty / Health & Beauty)

**Industry:** Beauty, Cosmetics, Skincare

The **Apex** preset is created for the beauty industry, combining soft, feminine aesthetics with a professional look that builds trust.

**Key features:**
- Pastels and rose gold accents
- Light, airy typography with generous line heights
- Soft edges and rounded corners
- Focus on product ingredients and benefits
- Trust badges and certifications

**Ideal for:**
- Cosmetics and makeup
- Skincare
- Perfumes and fragrances
- Hair care products
- Wellness and supplements

**Shopify Industry Tag:** `Beauty`

---

### Nexus (Electro / Electronics)

**Industry:** Electronics, Tech, Gadgets

The **Nexus** preset is designed for electronics and tech stores, offering a bold, modern design that communicates innovation and reliability.

**Key features:**
- Dark mode as default (optional)
- Bold typography with tight letter-spacing
- Vibrant tech accents (blue, cyan, neon)
- Product specifications in tech sheet format
- Sharp edges and geometric layouts

**Ideal for:**
- Consumer electronics
- Computers and components
- Smart home devices
- Audio and video
- Gaming gear

**Shopify Industry Tag:** `Electronics`

## Preset Structure

Each preset is defined in the `listings/` directory as a `listing.json` file:

```
listings/
├── aura/
│   └── listing.json    → Industry: "Clothing"
├── apex/
│   └── listing.json    → Industry: "Beauty"
└── nexus/
    └── listing.json    → Industry: "Electronics"
```

### Preset File Format

```json
{
  "industry": "Clothing"
}
```

The `industry` field must be one of the [allowed Shopify values](https://shopify.dev/docs/api/admin-graphql/latest/enums/AppSubscriptionLineItemType#field-shopifyappsubscriptionlineitemtype-industry).

## How to Apply a Preset

<Info>
  **Presets are set during theme installation.** You cannot change the preset after installation without reinstalling the theme.
</Info>

### During Theme Installation

1. Download Khors theme from the Shopify Theme Store
2. During the installation process, select the preset appropriate for your industry:
   - **Aura** for Fashion/Clothing
   - **Apex** for Beauty/Cosmetics
   - **Nexus** for Electronics/Tech
3. The theme will install with pre-configured settings for the selected industry

### After Installation

After installing a preset, you can customize all settings in the Theme Customizer:

- **Colors** - Change color schemes
- **Typography** - Select different fonts
- **Layout** - Adjust widths and margins
- **Sections** - Add, remove, reorganize sections

<Warning>
  Changing settings after installation does not change the assigned industry tag in `listing.json`. The industry tag only affects marketplace categorization.
</Warning>

## Customizing Presets

### Colors

Each preset has default color schemes that you can fully customize:

```
Aura (Fashion):
  Light: Off-white backgrounds, dark text, gold accents
  Dark: Black backgrounds, white text, gold accents

Apex (Beauty):
  Light: Cream/pink backgrounds, brown text, rose accents
  Dark: Deep plum backgrounds, cream text, rose gold accents

Nexus (Electro):
  Light: Light gray backgrounds, almost-black text, blue accents
  Dark: Almost-black backgrounds, white text, cyan accents
```

### Typography

Recommended fonts for each preset:

**Aura (Fashion):**
- Montserrat, Raleway (sans-serif)
- Playfair Display, Lora (serif)

**Apex (Beauty):**
- Inter, Nunito Sans (sans-serif)
- Cormorant Garamond (serif accent)

**Nexus (Electro):**
- Inter, Roboto, Space Grotesk (sans-serif)
- JetBrains Mono (monospace for specs)

### Layout

**Aura:** Generous whitespace, max-width 1200px
**Apex:** Soft, flowing layouts, max-width 1100px
**Nexus:** Tight, grid-based, max-width 1400px

## Best Practices

### Choosing the Right Preset

<Warning>
  Select the preset that **most closely** matches your primary product category. Mixing presets (e.g., fashion preset for electronics) reduces conversions.
</Warning>

**Helpful questions:**
- What is the primary product type? (70%+ inventory)
- What tone of voice does my brand have? (elegant vs. technical)
- What emotions do I want to evoke? (trust vs. excitement)

### Consistency After Selection

After selecting a preset:
1. **Stick to the chosen aesthetic** in product photos
2. **Match copy** to the preset style (elegant vs. technical)
3. **Use recommended fonts** for consistency
4. **Maintain whitespace proportions** recommended for the preset

### Cross-Category Stores

If you sell products from multiple categories:
- Choose the preset for the **dominant category** (>50% revenue)
- Or choose **Aura** as the most universal option

## FAQ

### Can I change the preset after installation?

Not directly. The industry tag in `listing.json` is set during installation and only affects marketplace categorization, not theme functionality. However, you can:
- Change all settings (colors, fonts, layout) in the Customizer
- Manually edit `listing.json` (requires developer access)
- Reinstall the theme with a different preset

### Does the preset affect theme functionality?

No. All presets have **identical functions** and features. They only differ in:
- Default colors
- Recommended typography
- Sample content
- Industry tag for Shopify

### Can I mix styles from different presets?

Yes, you can copy settings between presets in the Theme Customizer:
1. Note the settings from one preset (colors, fonts)
2. Apply them manually to your theme
3. Mix & match as needed

<Info>
  However, we recommend sticking to one consistent style for the best user experience and conversions.
</Info>
