## Overview

The Newsletter section helps you build your email list by adding a subscription form to any page. Subscribers are automatically added to your store's customer list with a "newsletter" tag for easy segmentation.

## Settings

### Content

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Heading** | Main title for the section (e.g., "Join our community") |
| **Description** | Rich text explaining the benefits of subscribing. Supports bold, italic, and links. |

### Style

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Color scheme** | Choose Light, Dark, or Accent to match your page design |

### Spacing

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Padding top/bottom** | Adjust vertical spacing from 0 to 100px |

## How It Works

1. **Customer enters email** — Form validates the email address
2. **Submission** — Email is sent to Shopify and tagged as "newsletter"
3. **Confirmation** — Success message appears with a checkmark icon
4. **Customer record** — Email appears in **Customers** section with "newsletter" tag

<Note>
Subscribers are stored in your Shopify customer database. You can export them or sync to email marketing apps like Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or Shopify Email.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<Check>Place newsletter section in the footer or at the bottom of your homepage for maximum visibility</Check>

<Check>Keep the description concise and benefit-focused (e.g., "Get 10% off your first order + exclusive updates")</Check>

<Check>Use the Accent color scheme to make the section stand out from page content</Check>

<Warning>
If you're targeting customers in the EU, consult your legal team about adding a checkbox for GDPR consent. The basic section collects emails only.
</Warning>

## Using Subscriber Data

### In Shopify Admin

Navigate to **Customers** and create a customer segment:
- Filter: "Customer tags" contains "newsletter"
- Save as "Newsletter Subscribers"

### With Email Marketing Apps

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Shopify Email">
    1. Go to **Marketing > Automations**
    2. Create customer segment with tag "newsletter"
    3. Build email campaigns targeting this segment
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Klaviyo">
    Klaviyo automatically syncs customers with "newsletter" tag to a dedicated list. Configure in Klaviyo app settings under Shopify integration.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mailchimp">
    Install the Mailchimp app and enable customer sync. Map "newsletter" tag to a specific Mailchimp audience in app settings.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## GDPR & Privacy

For compliance with data protection regulations:

1. Update your **Privacy Policy** (Settings > Policies) to mention newsletter collection
2. Add clear unsubscribe links in all email campaigns
3. Honor unsubscribe requests by removing the "newsletter" tag from customers

<Tip>
Shopify includes customer data management tools for GDPR compliance in **Settings > Customer privacy**.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Form not appearing">
    Check that the section is enabled in the theme customizer. Ensure padding settings aren't set to 0 on both top and bottom, which could make the section invisible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Emails not appearing in Customers">
    Verify submissions aren't being marked as spam. Check **Settings > Notifications > Customer notifications** to ensure emails can be collected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Success message not showing">
    This is a page reload issue. After submission, Shopify redirects back to the page with a success parameter. Ensure you don't have JavaScript interfering with form submission.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
