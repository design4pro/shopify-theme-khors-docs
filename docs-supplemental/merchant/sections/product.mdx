## Overview

The product section displays product images, variants, pricing, and add-to-cart functionality.

## Settings

### Media Gallery
Configure image layout and zoom options.

### Variant Selector
Choose between dropdown or button selectors.

### Quantity Selector
Enable or disable quantity input.

### Dynamic Checkout
Show accelerated checkout buttons (Shop Pay, Apple Pay).
