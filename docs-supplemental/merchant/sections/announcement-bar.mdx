## Overview

The announcement bar displays a banner message above your header. Use it for promotions, shipping information, or important store updates. Visitors can dismiss it, and their preference is remembered across visits.

## Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Text** | Your announcement message (supports rich text formatting) |
| **Link** | Optional URL — the entire message becomes clickable |
| **Open in new window** | Open the link in a new browser tab |
| **Dismissible** | Allow visitors to close the bar (default: on). Their choice is saved in the browser. |
| **Color scheme** | Light, dark, or accent (default: accent for visibility) |

## Tips

- Keep messages short and action-oriented: "Free shipping on orders over $50"
- Use the **accent** color scheme for maximum visibility
- If linking to a sale page, enable "Open in new window" so visitors don't lose their place
- The dismiss preference resets when you change the section settings in the Theme Editor
