## Overview

The cart section displays cart items, totals, and checkout options.

## Settings

### Cart Type
Choose between cart page or cart drawer.

### Cart Notes
Enable order notes field.

### Upsells
Show recommended products in cart.

### Free Shipping Bar
Display progress toward free shipping threshold.
